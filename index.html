hello, im hannan!

I'm a student at the University of California, Davis. Currently, I am pursuing a B.A in design and a B.S computer science.

I am a storyteller at heart. I love communicating narratives through illustrations, writing, and code Learning about people of different backgrounds and their stories excite me, and I try to design with a story in mind.
